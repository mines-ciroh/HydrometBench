Water Supply Forecast Rodeo Archive

This directory contains archived materials from the Bureau of Reclamation Water Supply Forecast Rodeo competition, including publicly released competition datasets and final winning team reports. These files were downloaded from the official competition repositories and webpages listed below and are archived here to support reproducibility and long-term preservation associated with the following manuscript:

A Community Evaluation Protocol for Seasonal Water Supply Forecasting in the Western U.S.
Joshua T. Sturtevant, Andrew W. Wood, Chris D. Frans, Ian M. Ferguson, and Thomas C. Pagano

Original Data Sources

Competition repository:
DrivenData Water Supply Forecast Rodeo GitHub Repository: https://github.com/drivendataorg/water-supply-forecast-rodeo/tree/main

Competition webpage:
Water Supply Forecast Rodeo Competition Page: https://www.drivendata.org/competitions/group/reclamation-water-supply-forecast/
